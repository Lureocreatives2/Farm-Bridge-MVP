Our Mission

Closing the Gap Between Farm and Table

Nigeria has one of Africa's most productive agricultural sectors, yet farmers often earn a fraction of what their produce is worth — while buyers pay inflated prices. The problem? Too many middlemen.

FarmBridge removes that gap. We give farmers a digital storefront and connect them directly with buyers via WhatsApp — the tool every Nigerian already uses daily.

No commissions. No complicated apps. No gatekeepers. Just farmers and buyers doing business the way it should be.
