Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get most often

For Buyers
How do I buy from a farmer? ▾
Browse our Shop page, click on any product to see full details, then click the green "Contact Farmer on WhatsApp" button. This opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message so you can negotiate price and arrange delivery directly with the farmer.
Does FarmBridge charge buyers any fees? ▾
No — FarmBridge is completely free for buyers. You pay the farmer directly; we don't process or take any cut from transactions.
How do I know the produce is fresh? ▾
Each listing includes a description from the farmer, which should include harvest date and quality details. We recommend asking the farmer directly via WhatsApp before committing to a large order. Always inspect goods on delivery before making payment.
What if a product listing looks fake or suspicious? ▾
Use the Contact page to report it to our admin team. Select "Report a Listing" as the subject and include the product name. Our team will investigate and remove it within 24 hours.
Can I search for produce in my state? ▾
Yes — on the Shop page use the state filter dropdown to see listings from farmers in a specific state.
For Farmers
How do I register as a farmer? ▾
Go to Register Your Farm, enter your name, phone number, farm name, and state. You'll receive a unique Farmer ID — save this, as you'll need it to list products.
Is it free to list my products? ▾
Yes, completely free. FarmBridge charges no listing fees and no sales commission. You keep 100% of what you earn from buyers.
How do buyers contact me? ▾
Buyers click the WhatsApp button on your product listing and are connected to you via your registered phone number. The message is pre-filled with your product name so you know exactly what they're interested in.
My listing is pending — when will it go live? ▾
All new listings go through a quick admin review before being approved and made visible to buyers. This usually takes less than 24 hours. We review for quality, accurate descriptions, and appropriate pricing.
Can I list multiple products? ▾
Yes — use the List a Product page to add as many listings as you like. Each product gets its own page with a direct WhatsApp contact button.
What makes a good listing? ▾
The best listings include: a clear real photo of the actual product, an accurate description with harvest date and quality details, your minimum order quantity, and packaging/delivery information. Listings with photos get significantly more buyer enquiries.
About FarmBridge
Does FarmBridge handle payments or delivery? ▾
Not currently. FarmBridge connects buyers and farmers directly — payment and delivery are arranged between the two parties via WhatsApp. This keeps things simple and means no platform fees for anyone. Payment and delivery features are planned for a future update.
Which states does FarmBridge cover? ▾
FarmBridge covers all 36 states and the FCT. Any Nigerian farmer can register and list products regardless of location.
How do I report a problem or give feedback? ▾
Use our Contact page. We read every message and usually respond within 24 hours on weekdays.
